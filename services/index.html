Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

How long before I see results?
Paid ads typically show traction within the first 2–4 weeks. SEO is a longer play — you'll see meaningful movement in 3–6 months, with compounding returns after that. We'll set honest expectations on your discovery call based on your specific situation.
Do I need all six services?
Not at all. Most clients start with one or two services that match their most immediate goal, then expand as they grow. We'll recommend only what actually makes sense for where your business is right now.
What makes Roccia different from other agencies?
No templates, no account managers reading from a script. Every strategy is custom-built for your business. You get direct access to the people doing the work, transparent reporting, and a team that treats your budget like it's their own.
Are there long-term contracts?
No lock-ins. We operate on monthly agreements because we believe our results should be what keeps you — not a contract. That said, marketing compounds over time, so clients who stick with us typically see the strongest returns after month 3.
What's the minimum budget for paid ads?
We recommend a minimum ad spend of $1,000/month to generate enough data to optimize properly. Below that, results are inconsistent. Our management fee is separate — we'll walk you through total investment on your call.
